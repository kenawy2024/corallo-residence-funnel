
**Context:** Used for cold traffic, ROI-focused. Pause pattern interrupt.

**Context:** Lifestyle variant. Emotional, dreamy tone. Opens with morning scene over Red Sea.

**Context:** Investment variant. Pattern disrupt. Contrast between passive dollar saving vs. active dollar earning.

## Ad Copy Notes (ملاحظات الكتابة الإعلانية)

### ✅ Best Practices Applied:
- **Arabic colloquial** (عامية مصرية): أكثر تفاعلاً وأقل رسمية
- **Emoji anchors**: تسهّل القراءة على موبايل
- **Short sentences**: مناسبة لـ Facebook/Instagram feed
- **Clear CTA**: كل إعلان ينتهي بدعوة واضحة للعمل
- **Benefit-first**: النص يبدأ بالفائدة للعميل مش بوصف المشروع
- **Social proof numbers**: 200+ وحدة، 90%+ إشغال

### 🎯 Targeting Suggestions:
**Cold Traffic (Prospecting):**
- Egypt, ages 28–55
- Interests: Real estate, investment, travel, beach, luxury lifestyle
- Custom: Lookalike of existing leads (1–3%)

**Retargeting:**
- Landing page visitors (all) → Last 14 days
- Video viewers 50%+ → Last 30 days
- Instagram engagement → Last 60 days
- WhatsApp link clickers → Last 30 days

### 📐 Ad Format Recommendations:
| Format | Best For | Copy Type |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| Single Image | Prospecting, Urgency | Texts 1, 3 |
| Carousel | Unit showcase, features | All units + CTA |
| Reel/Video | Hook + lifestyle | Hook 2 → Lifestyle |
| Story | Urgency, Scarcity | Headline 5 |
| Collection | Units browsing | Units + prices |

### 💰 Budget Guidelines:
- **Prospecting**: 60% of budget (cold audiences)
- **Retargeting**: 30% of budget (warm audiences)
- **Lookalike**: 10% of budget (after 100+ leads)
- **Test budget**: Minimum EGP 200/day per ad set
- **Scale trigger**: CPL below EGP 150 → double budget
